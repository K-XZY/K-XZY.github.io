Reading for Insights

Pay attention to when the authors explain why. For example:

要注意找作者解释为什么的时候. 比如说:

  • VL-JEPA: The authors explain why continuous features are used, and briefly explain why this makes learning simpler.
  • Attention is All You Need: The authors explain from the perspective of information propagation distance why attention is used instead of traditional recurrent architectures.
  • Infinity: The authors explain why using BSQ or LFQ can achieve an infinite token effect.
  • VL-JEPA: 作者解释了为什么要使用continuous feature？以及稍微解释了为什么这样让学习变得更简单。
  • Attention is all you need: 作者从信息传播距离的角度上解释了，为什么要使用 attention,而不是传统的Recurrent Architecture。
  • Infinity: 作者解释了，为什么使用了BSQ或者LFQ就可以达到一个无限token的一个效果。

Also pay attention to what the authors don't explain, and why they don't. There are several possibilities:

还要注意作者没解释什么. 以及为什么没解释。有几种可能：

  • It doesn't sound good to say it out loud → Potential opportunities and pitfalls!
  • They don't know either, just relying on intuition → Take note.
  • They think it's common sense or knowledge everyone knows → Ask Claude!
  • 说来不好听。→ 潜在的机会和pitfall！
  • 他也不知道，就靠intuition。→ 记下来。
  • 他觉得是个common sense或者大家都懂的知识。→ 问Claude!

Example: VQ-GAN vs JEPA

  • VQ-GAN latent space + Transformer = MASKGiT = MASKViT: They can generate images by iterating over the latent codebook.
  • JEPA encoder latent space + Transformer = JEPA + Decoder: They can generate images too by iterating over the latent continuous vectors.
  • VQ-GAN latent space + Transformer = MASKGiT = MASKViT; They can generate images by iterating over the latent codebook.
  • JEPA encoder latent space + Transformer = JEPA; Add a Decoder. They can generate images too by iterating over the latent continuous vectors.

They appear to be doing the same thing. But they're actually not!

看起来两个方法在做同样的事情。但其实并不是!

  1. MASKViT: The encoder only encodes appearance for reconstruction. The Transformer Predictor learns semantics + reasoning + understanding appearance for image generation.
  2. JEPA: The encoder primarily learns semantics. The predictor handles reasoning!
  3. MASKViT: Encoder只负责编码外观,以便于Reconstruction. Transformer Predictor负责学习Semantics + 推理Reasoning+理解外观用于生成图片.
  4. JEPA: Encoder主要学习Semantics。Predictor负责推理Reasoning！

What Makes a Great Paper

It's not enough to be enough. It's not enough to be enough.

A good paper doesn't necessarily need massive compute or extremely high scores. But it must have:

好的论文并不一定需要巨大的算力或者过高的跑分。但必须有：

  1. Clear insights: After reading, others must gain some understanding
  2. Concise methods: One more module is unnecessary, one less parameter and it doesn't work
  3. Reproducible: Such as clean open-source code and open data
  4. 明确的insight，别人看完一定要在理解上有所收获
  5. 精简的方法，多一个模块没必要，少一个参数就跑不成
  6. 可复制，比如代码清晰的开源，数据开源
